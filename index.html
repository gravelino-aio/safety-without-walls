What problems does Rythmos® solve?

The problem

Room-Bound Safety

Traditional systems often lose visibility when residents leave their room.

Alert Fatigue

Staff receive too many disconnected alerts without enough context.

Disconnected Tools

Separate systems create extra screens, manual handoffs, and slower response.

The solution

Safety Without Confinement

Maintain awareness across apartments, shared spaces, exits, and outside.

Safety Insights

Use activity trends and event history to make better community-wide decisions.

One Coordinated Workflow

Nurse calls, wander alerts, fall monitoring, location tracking - all in one place.

Smarter Alerts

Give staff the resident, location, event type, and priority before they respond.
